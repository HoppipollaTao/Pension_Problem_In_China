Pension Problem in China

China’s population is growing old at a pretty fast rate and its looming demographic shift presents considerable social and economic challenges, especially for the pension system. Because the number of Chinese retirees is fast increasing and the poor investment of pension fund in the past few years, some provinces in China alreay have been in a serious pension problem. Many chinese people now concern about their life after retirement.
